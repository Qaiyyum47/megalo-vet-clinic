Providing Expert Care, Even on Weekends

Directed by Dr. Haniza, who has been actively involved in veterinary medicine since 2017, the clinic is dedicated to providing high-quality care for animals. She began her career at the Batu Pahat, Johor branch before relocating to Bintulu, where she continues her passion for veterinary care.


Specializing in soft tissue surgery, Dr. Haniza holds a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) degree from Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM) Serdang, where she also completed her foundation studies. With over six years of experience, she remains committed to ensuring animal well-being through expert treatment and compassionate service.
